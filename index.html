Department Leadership

A Welcome Message to Our Future Engineers

Welcome to the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University of Missouri-St. Louis. As the Founding Chair, I am thrilled to lead this new and dynamic department at a pivotal moment in technological history.

Our mission is to provide an elite, forward-looking education that prepares students for the challenges of tomorrow. From the transformation of smart grid technologies and renewable energy systems to the frontiers of robotics and vehicle electrification, our curriculum is designed to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Here at UMSL, we combine rigorous academic theory with state-of-the-art laboratory experiences. We are committed to fostering a community of innovation where students are not just learners, but architects of the future.

Whether you are a prospective student exploring your passion for engineering or a current scholar looking to specialize in next-generation systems, I invite you to join us in shaping the world through electricity and technology.

Dr. Xin Wang
Founding Chair, UMSL EE
