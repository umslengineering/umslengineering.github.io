Professional Development

Beyond the Classroom

At UMSL Electrical Engineering, we believe education extends far beyond the lecture hall. We host a recurring series of events designed to prepare you for a high-impact career.

  • Monthly Industry Speaker Series: Hear from lead engineers at Boeing, Ameren, and local tech startups.
  • Career Prep Workshops: Portfolios, resume reviews, and mock technical interviews.
  • Annual Design Symposium: Showcase your senior design project to industry partners and alumni.
