IEEE Senior Member ASME Senior Member Outstanding Educator Award

Biography

Dr. Xin Wang is the Founding Chair and a tenured Full Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University of Missouri - St. Louis (UMSL). Before joining UMSL in 2025, he was a tenured Full Professor at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville (SIUE), where he also served as Chair of the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering.

He is a recognized leader in the field, having received the IEEE Saint Louis Section 2019 Outstanding Educator Award.

Research Interests

  • Power systems and Power electronics
  • Smart grid and Control problems
  • Robotics and Vehicle electrification
  • Electric machines and drives

Education

  • Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering Marquette University, USA (2011)
  • M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering Marquette University, USA (2008)
  • B.Sc. in Electrical and Communications Engineering Taiyuan University of Technology, China (2006)

Selected Publications

Dr. Wang has authored or co-authored more than 90 archival journal and conference publications.

  • "Advanced Control of Smart Grid Systems" - IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid
  • "Robotics and Automation in Vehicle Electrification" - Journal of Power Electronics
  • "Design of High-Efficiency Electrical Machines" - ASME Journal of Mechanical Design
