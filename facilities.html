Category 01

Circuits, Electronics & Embedded Systems

Foundational teaching and early research labs for analog/digital circuits and MCU development.

Core Equipment

  • Oscilloscopes (≥200 MHz; Tektronix / Keysight)
  • Arbitrary Function Generators
  • Triple-Output Bench Power Supplies
  • 6.5 Digit Multimeters
  • Digital Logic Analyzers
  • Weller/Hakko Soldering stations
  • Nexys A7 FPGA Trainer Boards: State-of-the-art AMD Artix™ 7 platforms ideal for advanced university ECE courses.
  • Analog Discovery Studio: Integrated portable analog circuits lab stations with comprehensive parts kits.
  • NI ELVIS III: Advanced engineering laboratory solution for project-based learning.
  • ARM Cortex-M, STM32, Arduino MCU platforms

  • Mixed-signal Oscilloscopes (up to 33GHz)
  • High-speed Signal Integrity Probes
  • Automated Python-based Test Benches
